UNLOCK SYSTEM (Milestone-Based)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
🔓 ACCESS       → Play 5 trivia Q's (Gate: Can't skip)
🥉 BRONZE       → 10 correct + 70% accuracy (Soft gate: Recommended)
🥈 SILVER       → 50 correct + bronze in 2 categories
🥇 GOLD         → 100 correct + silver + 80% category accuracy
💎 PLATINUM     → 200 correct + all badges + 80%+ overall

MINI-GAMES PRIORITY (Build Order)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

#1 WORDLE VARIANT (Sprint 1) — EFFORT: 1.5 sprints
   Guess 5-letter category names in 6 tries
   Mechanics: Green/Yellow/Gray feedback (standard Wordle)
   Scoring: 100 pts (≤3 tries), 50 pts (4-6), 0 pts (fail)
   Learning: Reinforces category mastery

#2 QUICK-FIRE (Sprint 2) — EFFORT: 1 sprint
   10 random Qs, 15 sec each, combo multiplier (2x after 3 correct, 3x after 5)
   Mechanics: Reuse existing trivia engine + timer
   Scoring: Base 10 × combo × (time_remaining / 15)
   Learning: Speed + accuracy under pressure

#3 MEMORY TILES (Sprint 3) — EFFORT: 1.5 sprints
   12 tiles (4×3), match Q&A pairs
   Mechanics: 3 difficulty levels (Easy: 8 tiles; Medium: 12; Hard: 16)
   Scoring: 50 base + 25 per 10 sec remaining
   Learning: Active recall & retention

#4 WORD SCRAMBLE (Sprint 4) — EFFORT: 2 sprints
   5 anagrams per round, 20 sec per word, fill-in or type
   Mechanics: Easy (3-letter), Medium (5-7), Hard (8+)
   Scoring: 10 pts/word + 5 pts per 5 sec remaining
   Learning: Spelling, vocabulary, problem-solving

#5 CHALLENGE MODE (DEFER to Phase 5.2) — EFFORT: 2 sprints
   Composite: 3 random mini-games, cumulative, 3-life health bar
   (Dependencies on all 4 mini-games must be stable first)

POWER-UP SYSTEM
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Currency: "Boost Points" (earned separately from main Points)

Types (Cost | Effect | Recharge):
  • Double Points  (10 pts) → 1 game: 2× scoring | Daily
  • Skip Question  (5 pts)  → Trivia: auto-correct | Per session
  • Extra Time     (8 pts)  → Mini-games: +10 sec | Daily
  • Hint Unlock    (3 pts)  → Trivia: reveal hint | Unlimited
  • Combo Freeze   (7 pts)  → Quick-Fire: maintain combo if fail | Per game

Earning:
  • Milestone rewards: 5-pt bundle at each badge tier
  • Streaks: 3-day streak = 1 free random power-up
  • Achievements: Beat personal best = +2 pts
  • Daily bonus: First game = +5 pts
